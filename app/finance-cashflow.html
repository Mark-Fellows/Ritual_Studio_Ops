Data-quality flags - read before relying on these numbers
Operating Expense bucket unexplained. Xero shows a catch-all "Operating Expense" of $36k-$90k/month with no breakdown - this almost certainly contains payroll/wages paid outside the Fitness Instructors bills account. The forecast's "Other operating costs" line is a placeholder estimate until the bookkeeper provides an account-level breakdown. Treat projected burn as indicative, not exact.
Xero P&L not yet meaningful. Most non-Stripe income sits in "Other Income" lump sums ($300k-$554k/month) - likely unreconciled bank-feed items (transfers, loan receipts), not trading income. A Stripe-to-Xero reconciliation (Synder or A2X) is required before the Xero P&L can be trusted. This dashboard deliberately uses Stripe settlements only.
Opening cash balance unknown. The model starts from the opening-cash assumption you enter. Obtain the actual Xero bank balance and set it - every projected balance shifts by that amount.
Rent basis. Palm Beach rent $9,438 confirmed (unknown whether gross or net of body corporate/rates/water). Both-studios months show ~$12,800; Robina adds ~$3,360. Confirm against the lease.
